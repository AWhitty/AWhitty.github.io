Remarkable.js

Remarkable.js (a prototype)

Realtime inline commenting for
thoughtful online content

I built Remarkable.js as my final project for CS 184. The project is a prototype of what inline commenting (comments on specific elements of an article, rather than the whole thing) might look like on mobile and desktop devices.

I intend to flesh this project out as a miniature realtime commenting service for smaller deployments, so stay tuned!

Balls (requires a multi-touch device)

Balls is a simple proof of concept for multi-touch interactions with the Box2D physics engine in a browser. I built this to learn how to leverage the multi-touch APIs on modern tablet devices and to learn a thing or two about Box2DWeb.

It's pretty addictive to fling the balls around, so grab your iPad and have some fun.
