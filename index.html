    In my neverending quest to make the perfect cocktail, I have been scraping ebooks to build a graph of (so far 3,000) cocktail recipes and their ingredients. I can't publish the dataset due to copyright, but I intend to share a summary eventually. Here's an example of the data processed with a community clustering algorithm.

  • Rhyme prediction

    I think we can all agree that Google's autocompletion is pretty spot-on most of the time. But what if it could rhyme? I'm implementing a modified version of the a rhyme detection algorithm described in this paper to quickly predict rhymes in context.

  • Pimiento

    Pimiento is an RGB LED controller I devloped for the Raspberry Pi with an interface built using Ionic. I also soldered together a small circuit to bridge the RPi and the LED strips. Really, I broke the original IR-compatible controller while examining it and had to build out a new way to turn the lights on.
