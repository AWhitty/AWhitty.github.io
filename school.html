School (things I do for a grade)

Keychord

Keychord is a velocity-sensitive QWERTY keyboard for desktop jamming. Through this project, I developed an inexpensive technique to turn most dome-switch keyboards into expressive and familiar musical instruments. I am still actively working on this project, but I will post a detailed write-up when I find the time.
